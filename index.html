My name is Maby Lomotos, and I am a second year psychology major at UCR. I am 19 years old, and I was born in the Philippines; however, I have been raised in Bakersfield, California since I was 6 years old. Some of my hobbies include sports especially basketball and flag football as well as traveling to different parts of the world with my family.

Currently, my experience in the psychology field has been participating in experiments and taking part in tests in order to provide results for the research assistants to analyze. I plan on applying for a lab at UCR that allows me to perform studies and gather data from the results of test subjects. I also took part in a 3 week long research project with UCR's Brain Game Center in which I played a variation of video games in order to test my cognitive ability and memory.

I plan on attending grad school in the future in order to further my education in the psychology field. I have always had an interest when it comes to being around children whether it's playing with them or simply being in the presence of them. I also grew up taking care of my younger siblings which I believe has helped shape my nurture skills; therefore, I want to earn my Masters and pursue a career that revolves around the studies of children more specifically on their behavior and perspective on certain subjects.
